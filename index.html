About Me

Roshan Shrestha, Founder, Chief Executive Officer

I am Roshan Shrestha the founder of Nepal Realistic Solution. Having years of experience in the management consulting industry, I am credited for guiding the clients to leave a mark in their respective businesses and market. I am a proactive and enthusiastic individual who oversees the development of business strategies and marketing initiatives that position companies for continued growth as they compete with emerging companies on different national and international platforms.

I am also a proud graduate holding an internationally recognized degree from the UK. Being graduated with honors in Doctorate and Masters in Business Administration, I have specialized in strategic management, marketing, finance, and human capital. Having a wide range of knowledge in academics coalesced with exposure both locally and internationally makes me a competent individual with versatile efficiency at the organizational, managerial, strategic, and operational levels. Besides, I am also a certified Management Auditor and specialized in standards that come under the International Organization of Standardization (ISO) like ISO 9001:2015, ISMS 27001:2013, OHSAS, FSMS, HACCP, and many more.

Show More…

While I was in the UK, I began to realize one key thing; the UK’s manufacturing sector contributes to around 20% of the overall GDP while 80% comes from services. I recognized that the UK makes the most of financial capital from selling a service rather than making a product. I had always been attentive to things around me and took this observation as an opportunity to start something in Nepal. However, before starting my own company, I observed the consulting industry in Nepal and made comparisons accordingly. My boldness and vision to initiate new thoughts and enforce innovative approaches through guided directions landed me to work for one of the top management consultancies in Nepal.

I am a self-made entrepreneur whose executive business started as an intern within a very globally competitive industry. By believing in myself and keeping a learning mindset, today I have expertise in ISO Certification, management, business, and HR consultation. I am also an experienced trainer with practiced leadership, possessing mastery in accounting and finance, research and development, and communication. After years of hard work and dedication, I have opened my corporation in Nepal. I can proudly say that I am now one of the very few youngest entrepreneurs in Nepal.

Being an inquisitive person drives me to learn new things, experiment, and explore my passion, which has turned out into different expertise.
